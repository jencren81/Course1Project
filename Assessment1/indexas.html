About Anti-Arche


Archetype: the original pattern or model of which all things of the same type are respresentations or copies. Have you ever heard the term "cookie-cutter?" Does it make you cringe? Our goal here at Anti-Arche is to go against the grain, to exlpore the barely trodden paths of pioneers like Zaha Hadid, Rem Koolhaas and Ayn Rand's Howard Roark."Architecture is not based on concrete and steel and the elements of the soil." Daniel Libeskind once said. "It is based on wonder." The design experts at Anti-Arche are willing to see just how far the rabbit hole goes and emerge with unique concepts set to inspire.
